You are a iPhone game designer. Your goal is to produce a game that is an evolving world driven by local AI on the phone itself. 

----------------
The game: 

You begin as are a character in a small village with 3 other villagers: A researcher, a farmer, and a worker. you can interact with each character and the characters can interact with each other. Each character has a specific function. 
- Researcher: when you interact with a researcher, you can either tell him about some science or technology you know about, or show them a picture from your camera. He then will go on the research this topic and see what they need to build the technology. 
- Farmer: when you interact with a researcher, you can either tell them about some plant or animal you know about, or show them a picture from your camera. He then will go on the research this topic and see what they need to grow the plants or find and care for the animal.
- Worker: you can tell a worker to go do a certain task that you would do and they will preform it. 

Characters should walk around usually near their home and randomly interact with other characters when they are close. 
more characters should arrive periodically. Most of the characters that arrive should be npcs that do not have a role, though they can still interact with other characters.

The world: starts as a grass plains biome surrounded by a dark haze. Then, at some point, new biomes will be discovered and they will be added to the map. the biomes should have different resources for construction and technology building, and climates for different plants and animals to live in. 

Notes--
the option to photograph something will only appear when you are talking to the farmer or researcher.

-----------------
The AI:

All character the dialogue, interactions, and memory will be created. The movement of them will not be dictated by AI. The farmer and researcher will use a vision model to classify the picture they are given. Both of these characters will have to decide what they need in order to get the item they are shown or told about. Then they will log the item and the requirements to build it. 

The art for the items will also be generated by a stable diffusion model. 

The biome characteristics will also be generated by AI, the AI dictates the materials found in the biome. 

------------------

The art style:
top-down retro pixel art, strongly inspired by 16-bit era RPGs and cozy life-sim games.

defining traits:

Chunky, readable sprites with simple silhouettes and very little fine detail
Limited color palette with soft, pastel-leaning tones
Clean outlines, but not harsh black outlines everywhere
Hand-placed pixel shading rather than smooth gradients
Cute chibi proportions for characters and animals
Tile-based environment design, like classic overworld maps
Whimsical village/fantasy vibe, with a warm and playful feel

The overall mood is very similar to a mix of:

SNES/GBA-style RPG overworld art
indie farming/adventure games
cozy fantasy pixel art

More specifically, I’d describe it as:

“Cozy top-down fantasy pixel art with a pastel 16-bit RPG aesthetic.”
